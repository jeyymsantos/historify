ANCIENT PERIOD

Ancient history covers all continents inhabited by humans in the period 3000 BC – AD 500. This period started all the development of civilizations through advancement in transportation, navigation, communication, and finding better ways of living as conservation of life.

MIDDLE PERIOD

The start of the Middle Ages was marred by massive invasions and migrations. Wars were prevalent during this time. As such, great technology was needed in the field of weaponry, navigation, mass food and farm production, and health.

This period is also known as the age of exploration because of major advances in scientific and technological development.

What do you think are these developments? Learn more by clicking the button below!

MODERN PERIOD

The booming world population during the nineteenth century onwards demanded that more goods be produced at a faster rate. People needed efficient means of transportation to trade more goods and cover a larger distance.

Industrialization takes place and this is the period where technological and scientific progress unfolded at an unimaginable speed.
